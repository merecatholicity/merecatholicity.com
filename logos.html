Logos & Verbum

Read the book inside Logos or Verbum Bible software.

Mere Catholicity is available as a Word document prepared for the Personal Books tool in Logos and Verbum. When you build it, the software links every Scripture reference to your own Bibles and adds the book to your library like any other resource, with its own table of contents and full search. Personal Books is a free feature of the desktop application for Windows and Mac.

  • Download for Logos & Verbum (Free)

How to install

  1. Open Logos or Verbum on your computer. Open the Tools menu, then Utilities, then Personal Books.
  2. Click Add book and enter the title, Mere Catholicity. The other fields can stay as they are.
  3. Click Add file and choose the downloaded Mere_Catholicity_Logos.docx.
  4. Click Build book and wait a moment while it compiles.
  5. Done. The book will always be available in your library from now on, on that computer and any other desktop install signed in to the same account.
