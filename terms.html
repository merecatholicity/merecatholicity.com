Terms & conds

The rule of this house is the Golden Rule. “Therefore all things whatsoever ye would that men should do to you, do ye even so to them: for this is the law and the prophets” (Matthew 7:12). Hold to that in the comments and everything below follows on its own.

This website is based in the United States of America, and its speech is protected by the freedom of speech. The comments are the words of their posters, not of this site, and the same freedom that protects them does not oblige us to host them. Keep the comments civil and moral. Argue as hard as you please about what is true. Do not post what Aristotle would not approve of, and if you are unsure what Aristotle would approve of, he left books.

Comments are pseudonymous. Your identity is a key that lives in your browser, and a lost key is a lost name, for we cannot recover what we never had. Technical details of each post, such as the network address it came from, are logged and visible to the site owners alone. Comments may be screened before they appear, and we may remove any comment, or any commenter, at our own judgment. Creating an identity is your agreement to all of the above.

This site is served to its audience, North America, Europe from the Atlantic to Russia, and Oceania with its island chains. Elsewhere it is withheld, a measure of security and not of exclusion.

Scripture quotations are from the King James Version unless otherwise noted.
