\noindent\emph{Do Lutheran and Reformed pastors, without bishops in the
old succession, have valid orders? The authors do not fully agree. One
reads the episcopate with St.\ Jerome, the ruling presbyterate, so that
presbyteral orders bear a real defect rather than nothing. The defect is
the milder verdict, for where Rome judges such orders null and void,
this reading holds them real. The other reads it with Newman, that the episcopate
is an authentic development, its seed planted in the apostles and its
growth received by the whole Body. Here is the evidence for both.}
